About Us

PT. BLAMBANGAN BAHARI SHIPYARD was established since 2021 as a Shipbuilding Shipyard and Shipping Consultant. Our company is focused on supporting customers to achieve their targets in ship design and production.

We are called to help build the national maritime world by becoming a provider of manufacturing services and shipping consultants. We synergize the ability of reliable professional experts to support PT. BLAMBANGAN BAHARI SHIPYARD to become a partner of various parties, both for the government and private sector.

BLAMBANGAN BAHARI SHIPYARD has completed the construction of hundreds of units of ships of various types and sizes such as patrol vessel, naval ship, fast patrol, speed boat, and speed boat. The ship built and repaired is made of fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP) and wood. PT. BLAMBANGAN BAHARI SHIPYARD also has reliable human resources, works efficiently and has high productivity in supporting new ship construction, ship repair and conversion according to customer needs. We continue to innovate developing prototype ships, adding facilities such as area expansion, dock extension, slipway, building graving dock, machining and automation facilities and strengthening research and development activities. PT. BLAMBANGAN BAHARI SHIPYARD is actively looking for new locations in the country to develop its presence as a national shipyard.
