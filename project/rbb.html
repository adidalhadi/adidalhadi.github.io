Overview

Rigid Bouyancy Boat (RBB) type Oil Combat, which is equipped with an oil dispersant pump system with the aim of discharging as soon as possible due to an oil spill at sea. With this tool, it is a mixture of emulsifier and solvent to help break down the oil into small droplets so as to minimize the pollution that occurs.
